Carson Curry

Welcome to my professional portfolio page. I am a writer, designer and coder currently navigating the wide world of full-stack web design. On this page you can find a number of projects I have worked on as part of the KU Coding Bootcamp, as well as websites I have designed professionally. I will continually populate this page with new projects and apps, as well as build a larger body of writing, art and media projects on the main CWCOriginals page (coming soon).

I attended the University of Kansas between 2008 and 2012, where I graduated with a B.A. in English, emphasizing in creative writing. I utilize my creative writing skills gained in my academic, personal and professional life in all aspects of my work and prioritize a clean design with well-developed content. If you are interested in contacting me regarding your website (either in management or design), please don't hesitate to contact me through email or phone or visit one of my social links listed below.
