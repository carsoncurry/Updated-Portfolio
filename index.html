Carson Curry

Welcome to my professional portfolio page. I am a writer, designer and coder currently navigating the wide world of full-stack web design. On this page you can find a number of projects I have worked on as part of the KU Coding Bootcamp, as well as websites I have designed professionally. I will continually populate this page with new projects and apps, as well as build a larger body of writing, art and media projects on the main CWCOriginals page (coming soon).
I was born and raised in the Kansas City area and have developed a great passion for movies and television, literature, comics (Spider-Man and the X-Men, all the way), music of nearly all genres (though as a child of the 90s I would be remiss not to mention my preference for classic hip-hop), video games and sports. As a true Kansas Citian I've experienced the high highs of watching a Royal World Series and the constant struggle of cheering on the Chiefs come playoffs.

I attended the University of Kansas between 2008 and 2012, where I graduated with a B.A. in English, emphasizing in creative writing. I utilize my creative writing skills gained in my academic, personal and professional life in all aspects of my work and prioritize a clean design with well-developed content. If you are interested in contacting me regarding your website (either in management or design), please don't hesitate to send me a message using the contact form below.
